FONA MY CROWN

MY CROWN DESIGN / MY CROWN SCANNER / MY CROWN MILL

  • Product Specification
  • Features
  • Ask a Question

ABOUT


FONA MyCrown is a complete chairside CAD/CAM System allowing to create beautiful crowns and more in a single patient visit. The advanced software with guided steps makes the process effortless and easy.


COMPOSITION / UNITS

MyCrown Design / MyCrown Scanner

Description

  • Full chairside indications that includes inlays, onlays, Crowns, Veneers and Bridges. (Design up to full arch - Direct mill by MyCrown milling unit up to 40 mm Bridge).
  • Easy to use: 21" Touch screen, to be used like Ipad, can be used with Gloves.
  • Hygienic design the units are easy to maintain due to minimalist design with recommended use of antibacterial wipes or high level disinfection liquid. The camera is used in combination with single- use hygiene sheaths to protect the dentist and patient.
  • Intuitive user interface Software The user interface is clearly structured and employs self-explanatory icons.
  • Direct modification on the model : All tools are available to secure all modifications can be done on restorations.
  • Multiple restorations Depending on the given treatment situation, you can work simultaneously on several different restorations. When performing a complete anterior reconstruction, for example, you have the option to design veneers, crowns and bridges in parallel.
  • Natural Shape with just one click: Each patient will have his specific design according to neighbouring teeth, not from a library inside the system, as it is generated from the scanned data.
  • Adjustable restorations parameters enabling the user to adjust the required minimal thickness, proximal contacts strength, occlusal contacts strength, and the adhesive gap and Spacer of a restoration.
  • Imaging Technique and Area of application:
    • Continuous color video streaming for single tooth, quadrant, full jaw.
    • Very thin layer of titanium oxide spray MyCrown HD Spray on the area of interest.
    • Very easy to handle camera like a pen, with a very light camera
  • Open STL File export: Export STL file at any phase, acquisition data, model or restoration itself.


MyCrown Mill

Description

  • Milling accuracy tested and very accurate restoration.
  • High Speed milling coupled with optimum precision, e.g. crowns can be completed in just 10 to 14 minutes.
  • Wide range of indications one machine caters for all indications (inlays, onlays, crowns, veneers and bridges up to 40mm) and materials (feldspar/glass ceramics, lithium disilicate, Resins, wax, composites and zirconium oxide).
  • Two motors working simultaneously with automatic indication when the burs needs to be changed.
  • Easy to install with no necessary water supply plugs, compressors or separate computers.
  • Automatic indication when the wrong bloc is fixed, and when the water or the water filter needs to be exchanged.
  • Wireless and wired connections possible connection can be wired via LAN cable or dedicated Radio module.
  • Accepts different bur sizes according restoration type/Material.
  • Verified Open system accepting blocs from different manufacturers (Sirona, DENTSPLY, Vita,..)

Highlights

  • Fully integrated system
  • Fast and easy to use
  • Grow your clinic

Specification

No need for conventional impressions

The intuitive scan and MyCrown design software eliminate the need for conventional impressions, waiting time and further appointments.

Restoration accuracy and control

The state-of-the-art camera scans the area and advanced software produces a design with high accuracy and precision while, at the same time, allowing making adjustments for a perfect fit.

Freedom to choose material

With MyCrown Mill you are not limited to working with a specific manufacturer or material. The restoration blocks can be selected to suit your workflow and specific needs.

Save and share your design

The system allows to export the finished design for future reference and easily share it within the clinic or multiple offices.

Crowns and more in one visit

The MyCrown fully integrated CAD/CAM solution is able to create crowns, veneers, inlays, on lays and small bridges in just one visit. Choose the restoration block to be inserted to allow the use of preferred material and customize aesthetics to fit the patient.

Integrated system

The fully integrated complete system ensures high precision and smooth workflow due to steady communication between the MyCrown Scan, MyCrown Design software and MyCrown Mill.

HD spray

MyCrown HD spray applies a thin layer of black and white particles that are traced and measured to produce a precise model with color of the restoration without reflections.

Saving time

The full process of restoration is completed in a single appointment thus saving time for both patient and dentist.

Patient comfort

Unlike alternative impression-taking methods the process is efficient and the patient feels minimal discomfort. Scanning the restoration area with the compact camera is smooth and fast. The efficient treatment and lasting results persuade more and more patients to prefer one visit restorations to alternative options.

3D Image Acquisition and 2D Intraoral Camera in One Device

MyCrown comes with a camera that can be used in 2D and 3D mode. It can be used as a 2D intraoral camera thanks to the built-in color sensor, improving dentist-patient communication. Switching from 3D scan to 2D photos and videos is done easily, by the click of a button.

Hygiene

The units are easy to maintain due to minimalist design with recommended use of antibacterial wipes or high level disinfection liquid. The camera is used in combination with single-use hygiene sheaths to protect the dentist and patient.
